About Me

Learning and connecting with others. Creating experiences. Encouraging others to be their best and true selves.

With all of these in perfect harmony, I deliver solutions that help others script a better future.

I am a recent graduate of Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT)'s Computer Science program, with an immmersion in French. While at RIT, I served on numerous executive boards catering the minority student experience at RIT. 

In August 2018, I joined Accenture's Technology Development Program (TDP) as an Application Development Associate. I have since become an ICAgile Certified Professional (ICP), as well as a Certified Cloud Practioner in Amazon Web Services (AWS). I am currently studying AWS architecture as continuation of my AWS journey.

I have employed Accenture's large Corporate Citizenship program, and am on track to meet my goal of 25 hours of volunteering by my 1-year anniversary.

My previous work experience includes creating plug-ins for Microsoft Outlook and Autodesk AutoCAD, as well as developing software for Microsoft HoloLens platform.
